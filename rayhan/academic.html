Academic Information

Student who have passed ssc and hsc or any equivalent public examination with at a minimum gpa of 2.5 in each (5.00 scale) may apply for admission. student having a gpa of 2.00 in any one of ssc or hsc but a total of gpa 6.00 may also apply

For english medium student a minimum of subject in o level with a minimum gpa of 2.5 and 2 subjects in 'A' level with a minimum gpa of 2.5 is required. At least grade B or GPA 4.0 in four subject out of the seven subject and at least grade c or gpa 3.5 in the remaining three subject (A=5,b=4,C=3 &E=1) are required

The children of freedom fighters with a total of gpa 5.0 in both ssc and hsc or equivalent public examination may apply for admission with the freedom fighter certificate of the government.

  • For GED student, UGC guidelines wil be followed.
  • individual department may have additional requirement for a student to be eligible foe admission.
  • Students from Science,Business,Studies,Humanities or any equivalent background may apply for BBA,LLB(Hons),English(Hons),Economics(Hons) and jurnalism.But student who wish to have admission in cse or EEE must have a science background or any equivalent background with Math/Physics/Computer Studies.
